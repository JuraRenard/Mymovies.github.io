Why Mechatronics?

The Department of Mechatronics Engineering at Coimbatore Institute of Engineering and Technology (CIET) offers a dynamic and enriching environment, equipped with cutting-edge facilities, experienced faculty members, and a serene academic atmosphere. The department emphasizes the development of both technical expertise and soft skills, focusing on employability and communication abilities to ensure the holistic growth of its students. With a strong emphasis on discipline and values, the Department of Mechatronics Engineering aims to shape students into self-reliant, confident professionals, excelling not only in their academic pursuits but in all aspects of life.
